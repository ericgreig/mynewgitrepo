Tour De Giro is an online bike-racing simulation that uses real power inputs such as those from ANT+ power meters, computrainers, or power estimations from normal trainers (requires a inexpensive speed/cadence sensor).  Those power inputs are sent to a virtual rider on a server, allowing you to race head-to-head with other people across the planet.  The goal of Tour De Giro is to accurately simulate cycling road racing so that you can race with other people while you train.  

Tour De Giro is in beta right now, and we welcome feedback.  If you want a copy of the client to try out, email tourdegirodev@gmail.com for a copy.

Real Drafting Tactics

Tour de Giro simulates real world drafting conditions and all the tactics that come with the sport of cycling.

Huge Fields

Up to 60-70 riders cycling simultaneously. In game cyclists can be users from all over the world or intelligently crafted AI driven to beat you to the finish line.

Play with all your friends

Compete locally at home or in a studio against your friends.

Supports power meters of all types

ANT+ Quarq, Powertap, SRM, Computrainer, and speed/cadence sensors (on certain trainers) are all supported
